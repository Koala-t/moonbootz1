Diving the Deep Blue Sea

This is a very special webpage all about scuba diving!

Why do I love scuba diving?

  • When the water is crystal clear it feels like you're floating in space
  • You get to experience another whole world that is completely different than ours
  • Some of the best dive spots are also some of the world's most beautiful places

What are my top 3 dives?

Although I'm still very new to diving, I've been lucky enough to dive in some amazing places.

  1. White Rock, Koh Tao, Thailand
  2. Cenotes, Playa del Carmen, Mexico
  3. Tortugas, Cozumel, Mexico
